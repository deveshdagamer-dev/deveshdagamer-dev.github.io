Experimental methods and materials systems

A concise overview of technical expertise in synthesis, crystallography, magnetic measurements, spectroscopy, catalysis, and layered materials.

  • Single-crystal growth of halide double perovskites
  • Solid-state, solution-based, solvothermal, and hydrothermal synthesis
  • Slow cooling and temperature-gradient control
  • Schlenk-line and air-sensitive material handling experience

Structural characterization

  • Single-crystal X-ray diffraction
  • Powder X-ray diffraction
  • Structure solution and crystallographic refinement

Crystallographic tools

Magnetic measurements

  • Curie-Weiss analysis
  • Magnetic exchange and structure-property interpretation

Optical characterization

  • Band-gap analysis
  • Photoluminescence spectroscopy exposure
  • Optical response in low-dimensional halide systems

Catalysis and HER

  • Electrocatalytic hydrogen evolution reaction
  • Photocatalytic H2 evolution
  • TMD nanocomposite design
  • Activity comparison and structure-performance analysis

Materials systems

  • MoS2, MoSe2, MoTe2, and WS2
  • Mo2C and TMD-based nanocomposites

Layered and low-dimensional materials

  • TMD nanosheets and phase-controlled chalcogenides

Data analysis and writing

  • Research manuscript preparation
  • Scientific presentation and poster preparation
