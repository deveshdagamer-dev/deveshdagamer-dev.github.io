title: "Hydrogen Evolution Reaction (HER)"
summary: |
  Early work on phase-engineered MoS₂, MoSe₂, WS₂ and their nanocomposites established structure-activity relationships for electrocatalytic and photocatalytic hydrogen evolution. The cover article in ACS Applied Energy Materials demonstrated that 1D MoS₂ nanocomposites with polymer-functionalized carbon and borocarbonitride nanotubes exhibit markedly enhanced HER activity compared with pristine MoS₂.
