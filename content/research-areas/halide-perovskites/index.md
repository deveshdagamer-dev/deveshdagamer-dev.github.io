title: "Halide Double Perovskites"
summary: |
  My doctoral research introduced Mo(III) as the trivalent B-site cation in lead-free halide double perovskites, expanding the family across 0D, 1D, 2D and 3D structural connectivities. These materials exhibit rich magnetic exchange interactions and tunable optical absorption, and were crystallographically characterized by single-crystal X-ray diffraction. Publications in Chemical Science (HOT Article), Chemistry of Materials, Chemical Communications, and a manuscript under review at Angewandte Chemie International Edition.
