          <div class="ra-card">
            <h3>Halide Double Perovskites</h3>
            <p>Doctoral research introducing Mo(III) as the trivalent B-site cation in lead-free halide double perovskites across 0D, 1D, 2D and 3D structural connectivities. These materials exhibit rich magnetic exchange interactions and tunable optical absorption, characterised by single-crystal X-ray diffraction. Publications in <em>Chemical Science</em> (HOT Article &amp; Pick of the Week), <em>Chemistry of Materials</em>, <em>Chemical Communications</em>, and a manuscript under review at <em>Angewandte Chemie International Edition</em>.</p>
          </div>
          <div class="ra-card">
            <h3>Hydrogen Evolution Reaction (HER)</h3>
            <p>Phase-engineered MoS₂, MoSe₂, WS₂ and their nanocomposites for electrocatalytic and photocatalytic hydrogen evolution. The cover article in <em>ACS Applied Energy Materials</em> demonstrated that 1D MoS₂ nanocomposites with polymer-functionalized carbon and borocarbonitride nanotubes exhibit markedly enhanced HER activity compared with pristine MoS₂.</p>
          </div>
          <div class="ra-card">
            <h3>Transition Metal Dichalcogenides (TMDs)</h3>
            <p>Synthesis, structural characterisation and catalytic properties of 3R-polytype MoS₂, WS₂ and NbS₂ nanosheets. The 3R-polytype retains broken inversion symmetry, giving distinct electronic and catalytic behaviour relative to the 2H phase. Studies conducted in collaboration with Prof. C.N.R. Rao's group at JNCASR.</p>
          </div>
          <div class="ra-card">
            <h3>Crystal Structure–Property Relationships</h3>
            <p>Single-crystal X-ray diffraction (SCXRD) used to establish precise structure–property relationships. Structural dimensionality control in Mo(III) halide perovskites tunes magnetic exchange pathways and optical band gaps. Halide substitution (Cl/Br) enables continuous modulation of magnetic coupling constants validated by DFT and SQUID magnetometry.</p>
          </div>
