title: "Crystal Structure–Property Relationships"
summary: |
  A unifying theme across my research is the use of single-crystal X-ray diffraction (SCXRD) to establish precise structure–property relationships. Structural dimensionality control in Mo(III) halide perovskites tunes magnetic exchange pathways and optical band gaps. Halide substitution (Cl/Br) in 1D perovskites enables continuous modulation of magnetic coupling constants and optical absorption edges, validated by DFT calculations and SQUID magnetometry.
