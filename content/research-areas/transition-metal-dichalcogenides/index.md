---
title: "Transition Metal Dichalcogenides (TMDs)"
summary: |
  Work on 3R-polytype MoS₂, WS₂ and NbS₂ nanosheets addressed their synthesis, structural characterization, and electrocatalytic/photocatalytic properties. The 3R-polytype retains broken inversion symmetry, giving rise to distinct electronic and catalytic behavior relative to the 2H phase. These studies were conducted in collaboration with Prof. C.N.R. Rao's group at JNCASR.
