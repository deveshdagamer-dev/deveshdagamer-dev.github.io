About

I am a materials chemist specialising in the synthesis and characterisation of magnetic halide double perovskites and transition metal dichalcogenides, with a PhD from JNCASR, Bangalore, India, where I worked under the mentorship of world-leading researchers in solid-state chemistry. My research bridges fundamental magnetism and functional catalysis, combining single-crystal X-ray diffraction, magnetic measurements, and electrochemical methods to understand structure-property relationships in emerging inorganic materials. Open to postdoctoral opportunities in solid-state chemistry, magnetic materials, and functional inorganic materials.

Research Highlights

Halide Double Perovskites

Rational design of Mo(III)-based halide double perovskites with tunable magnetic exchange interactions, probing structure-property relationships via SCXRD, magnetic susceptibility, and diffuse reflectance spectroscopy.

Key result: First Mo(III) B-site halide double perovskites with long-range magnetic ordering reported in Chemical Science.

Magnetic Exchange Interactions

Investigation of super-exchange and direct exchange pathways in 3D perovskite frameworks, combining DC/AC magnetometry with density functional theory to map spin Hamiltonians and critical temperatures.

Key result: Established quantitative exchange parameters governing antiferromagnetic ground states in chloride and bromide halide systems.

TMDs for Hydrogen Evolution

Scalable synthesis of MoS2, MoSe2, WS2, and Mo2C electrocatalysts with controlled morphology and edge-site density, benchmarked against Pt/C standards in acidic media for the hydrogen evolution reaction.

Key result: Achieved competitive overpotentials with engineered defect-rich nanostructures and nanocomposites with carbon and BN nanotubes.

Single-Crystal Growth & SCXRD

Expertise in Bridgman, chemical vapour transport, and flux methods for growing phase-pure single crystals of halide perovskites and layered chalcogenides; structure solution and refinement using SHELX/OLEX2.

Key result: Solved and published multiple novel crystal structures determining unprecedented coordination environments for Mo(III).
