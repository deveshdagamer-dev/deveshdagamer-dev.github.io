Experimental materials chemistry

Dr. Devesh Chandra Binwal

Experimental Materials Chemist | Halide Perovskites | Crystal Growth | Magnetism | TMDs/HER

I am a Research Associate at the Jawaharlal Nehru Centre for Advanced Scientific Research (JNCASR), Bengaluru, India. I completed my PhD in Materials Science in 2025 under the supervision of Prof. C. N. R. Rao, FRS, and Dr. Pratap Vishnoi.

My research focuses on crystal growth, structure determination, and structure–property relationships in functional inorganic and hybrid materials, particularly halide perovskites, magnetic materials, and layered transition-metal dichalcogenides.
